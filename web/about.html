About the Artist

Painting is
how I listen
to the world

I'm Ananya — a self-taught fine artist based in Kathmandu, Nepal. For over twelve years, I've been translating the light, silence, and quiet chaos of everyday life into oil and acrylic on canvas.

My studio sits where the Himalayan morning fog meets the city's warm clatter. That tension — between stillness and noise, between the ancient and the immediate — is what I paint.

My Philosophy

Art Should Feel Like Coming Home

I don't paint to impress — I paint to connect. Every brushstroke is a question I'm asking the canvas: what does this color feel like? Where does the light want to go?

I believe that a painting earns its place on your wall when it makes you stop, even for half a second, every time you pass it. That pause — that tiny moment of recognition — is what I'm after.

My work moves between representational and abstract, always rooted in emotion first and technique second. Form follows feeling.

Authenticity

Every work is hand-painted, signed, and shipped with a certificate of origin. No prints. No mass production.

Slow Art

I take the time a piece needs — sometimes weeks. There are no shortcuts in the relationship between artist and canvas.

Accessibility

Fine art shouldn't live only in galleries. I price thoughtfully so that real homes can hold real paintings.

The Journey

A Life in Paint

2010

The First Canvas

Gifted a set of oil paints at age 16. Painted for 6 hours straight and knew — this was it. That first piece (terrible, beautiful) still hangs in my mother's kitchen.

2013

First Exhibition

Participated in a group show at Nepal Art Council, Kathmandu. Sold my first painting for 3,000 NPR — spent it immediately on more paint and canvas.

2016

Residency in Rajasthan

A month-long artist residency in Jaipur introduced me to miniature painting traditions that permanently changed how I think about detail, patience, and restraint.

2019

Solo Exhibition

First solo show — "Fog & Light" — exhibited 28 original works. Every piece sold. A moment I'll never forget.

2021

Online Studio Launch

Launched Palette & Soul online, opening commissions globally. Connected with collectors in 15 countries across Europe, North America, and South Asia.

2024

Teaching & Today

Now running monthly workshops from my Kathmandu studio, continuing to commission, paint, and grow. More than 340 original works created to date.
